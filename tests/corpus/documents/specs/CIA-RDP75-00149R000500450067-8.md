(//JOINT TS AUS CAN ITA USA//REL TO USA, AUS, CAN, ITA, EU) White House Denies Nixon's Cuba Charge

(TS//SI/TK-BLFH//SAR-BP 52X3//RS/NF) By E. W. KENWORTHY Special to The New York Times.

(//JOINT TS AUS CAN ITA USA//REL TO USA, AUS, CAN, ITA, EU) WASHINGTON, March 20 The White House denied today a charge by Richard M. Nixon that President Kennedy had been informed of the secret preparations for a refugee landing in Cuba before he issued his 1960 campaign call for United States aid to anti-Castro forces.

(TS//SI/TK-BLFH//SAR-BP 52X3//RS/NF) Allen W. Dulles, who was head of the Central Intelligence Agency at the time, supported the President's reconstruction of events, but said he believed Mr. Nixon's statement to be the result of "an honest misunderstanding."
