(//NS//REL TO USA, NATO) Kim Deok

(TS//SI-G ABCD//OC/REL TO USA, NOR) First person without military or security background to assume post; position downgraded from level of deputy prime minister to rank of minister by President Kim . .. served as adviser to Kim during presidential campaign . . professor of international politics at Hankuk University of Foreign Studies during 1974 - February 1993 . . . well versed in North Korean affairs and international politics, especially issues relating to China and Russia, according to South Korean press . . . holds bachelor's degree from Seoul National University's School of Law, master's degree from Indiana University, Ph.D. in political science from Hankuk University . . . author of Diplomacy of Small and Weak Powers . . . is from North Kyongsang Province . . . born 25 May 1935.
